Subdomain Setup

Your storefront lives on a subdomain of your existing website — no changes to your main site needed.

🔧 Option A: We Do It For You

Give us temporary access to your domain hosting (GoDaddy, Cloudflare, Namecheap, etc.) and we'll add the CNAME record to point your subdomain to BigCommerce. Takes 5 minutes.

📄 Option B: We Send Instructions

We send you step-by-step instructions with the exact CNAME record to add. Your IT person or hosting provider can do it in minutes. We verify once it's done.

How It Works Technically

You add a CNAME record for your chosen subdomain (e.g. orders.yourshop.com) that points to your BigCommerce store URL. BigCommerce handles SSL automatically. Your main website at yourshop.com is completely unaffected — this is an independent store on a subdomain.

BigCommerce Account

You create and own your own BigCommerce account. We build on top of it.

💳 You Pay BigCommerce Directly

BigCommerce Standard plan is \$39/month, billed directly by BigCommerce. This covers your hosting, SSL, checkout, and order management. You own the account.

🔑 Full Admin Access

You get full admin access to your BigCommerce dashboard. View orders, manage customers, process refunds, run reports — it's your store. We just build it for you.

⚠️ Don't Have a BigCommerce Account Yet?

No problem. We'll walk you through creating one during the setup process. It takes about 10 minutes. You'll need a business email and payment method for the \$39/month hosting fee (paid to BigCommerce, not us).

Additional Costs

Transparent breakdown of everything you'll pay.

💳 BigCommerce Hosting

\$39/month

Paid directly to BigCommerce. Covers hosting, SSL, checkout, order management, and customer accounts. You create and own the account.

💸 Payment Processing

~2.9% + \$0.30

Standard payment processing fees from your chosen gateway (Stripe, PayPal, Square, etc.). Paid directly to your processor — we don't touch payments.

Total Monthly Cost Example

With the Managed plan: \$39 (BigCommerce) + \$250 (BulkPrintPro maintenance) = \$289/month total. Plus standard payment processing fees on orders. That's it — no hidden fees, no per-order charges, no revenue share.

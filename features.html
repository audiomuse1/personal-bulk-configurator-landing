Features

Everything your print shop needs to take bulk orders online — built in from day one.

Instant Bulk Pricing

Customers see your real prices — live — as they configure their order. No waiting for a quote. No back-and-forth. Your pricing tiers, your margins, calculated automatically.

  • Tiered quantity pricing (e.g. 24–47 = \$8.50, 48–99 = \$7.25)
  • Price updates instantly as sizes and quantities change
  • Per-unit and total price displayed clearly
  • Your rate sheet built into the system — we configure it for you
  • Pricing pulled from a Google Sheet you can update anytime

Smart Artwork Upload

Stop getting low-res JPEGs that your designer has to fix. The upload step validates files before they're submitted and gives customers clear guidance.

  • Automatic DPI detection — flags low-resolution files
  • File type validation (PNG, PDF, AI, EPS, SVG accepted)
  • Per-product artwork specs (dimensions, bleed, color mode)
  • Drag-and-drop upload with preview
  • Vector file badge for print-ready formats
  • Front and back artwork for two-sided products

Live Mockup Preview

Customers see their artwork on the actual product — in the color they selected — before placing an order. Less confusion, fewer revisions.

  • Product shown in selected garment color
  • Artwork overlaid in the correct print zone
  • Drag to reposition, corner handle to resize
  • Front and back views for two-sided prints
  • Side-by-side layout for unisex + women's fits
  • Placement coordinates captured with order

Guided Product Configurator

Each product type has its own step-by-step wizard. Customers can't miss a field or submit an incomplete order.

  • Step-by-step wizard tailored to each product type
  • Color swatches with 30+ options for garments
  • Size-by-size quantity entry
  • Print location selection (front, back, chest, etc.)
  • Optional union label add-on
  • Order notes field for special instructions

Your Brand, Your Store

Every storefront is customized to match your shop's identity. Your customers see your brand throughout — from the homepage to the checkout receipt.

  • Your logo in header and footer
  • Custom color scheme (accent, background, buttons)
  • Your shop name and branding throughout
  • Lives on your subdomain (orders.yourshop.com)
  • We handle CNAME setup or give you instructions
